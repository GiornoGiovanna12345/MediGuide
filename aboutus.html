Who We Are

MediGuide is a web-based healthcare advisory platform designed to bridge the gap between individuals and accessible health guidance. We provide simple, reliable tools to help you understand your symptoms, manage your appointments and assess your overall wellness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make basic healthcare guidance accessible to everyone. We believe that every individual deserves quick, clear and reliable health information without the need for an immediate doctor's visit.

What We Offer

MediGuide offers three core features — a Symptom Checker that recommends the right specialist for your concerns, an Appointment Booking system that saves your complete medical history, and a Wellness Score checker that evaluates your lifestyle and gives personalised tips.

Disclaimer
